# FloraNest Pro 
### A minimalistic Plant Scanning IOS application. <br>

- Floranest is a plant application designed to comply with daily life tasks allowing users to scan or upload images of indoor and outdoor plants to recieve detection data, health and care plans for the plants.
- The design language has been modeled to prioritise user functionality and minimalism to reduce distractions whilst providing pleanty of features.
- AI image detection allows for niche and common plants to be easily identified with backend API's form credited sources and libraries. 

# Features
- Scan or upload plants from camera roll for feedback and plant infomation.
- Detection of common plant diseases and reccomened treatment. 
- Personalised Plant care reccomendations for long term plant maintainance.

## Development. 
Developed in Xcode using Swift as the primary language.
